Why Peru?

The third largest country in South America

Peru is a land of ancient history, stunning landscapes, and vibrant culture. From the breathtaking ruins of Machu Picchu to the colorful streets of Cusco and the vast Amazon rainforest, Peru offers adventure at every turn. Savor world-renowned cuisine, explore diverse ecosystems, and immerse yourself in the rich traditions of the Andes. Whether you're trekking the Inca Trail or cruising on Lake Titicaca, Peru promises an unforgettable journey.

Where to go

Best Places to visit in Peru

Hike the Iconic
Inca Trail

The Inca Trail is one of the world’s most iconic hikes, leading adventurers through breathtaking Andean landscapes, ancient Incan ruins, and lush cloud forests. This legendary trek culminates at the awe-inspiring Sun Gate, offering a stunning first glimpse of Machu Picchu. With its rich history and incredible scenery, the Inca Trail is a must for travelers seeking adventure and a deep connection to Peru’s past.

Learn more

Wander through the colourful streets of Cusco

Cusco, the historic heart of the Inca Empire, is a vibrant city filled with culture, history, and stunning architecture. Wander through the cobbled streets of San Blas, explore the impressive Sacsayhuamán ruins, and visit the bustling San Pedro Market for local flavors. Discover the blend of Incan and colonial influences in the Plaza de Armas, and experience Cusco’s lively nightlife. As the gateway to Machu Picchu, Cusco is a must-visit destination rich in charm and history.

Learn more

Visit the mesmerising Lake Titicaca

Lake Titicaca, the highest navigable lake in the world, is a breathtaking destination steeped in history and culture. Explore the floating Uros Islands, made entirely of reeds, and visit Taquile and Amantani for a glimpse into traditional Andean life. Surrounded by stunning landscapes and rich legends, the lake is a serene yet fascinating place where ancient traditions thrive amidst the shimmering blue waters.
